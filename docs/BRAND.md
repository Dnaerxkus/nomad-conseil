# Brand Guide — Nomad Conseil

## Tone of voice
- **Expert, not showy.** The work speaks for itself. No superlatives.
- **Formal but not cold.** Institutional precision with human clarity.
- **Concise.** Short sentences. Active voice. No jargon without explanation.
- **Bilingual parity.** Every sentence must exist in both FR and EN. Translations are not literal — they are adapted for each audience.

## Colour palette

| Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Stone | `#f2ede6` | Page background — warm off-white |
| Void | `#0f0f0d` | Primary text — near-black warm |
| Concrete | `#1e1d1a` | Dark section backgrounds (footer, CTA) |
| Ash | `#8a8780` | Muted text, placeholders, borders |

## Logo
- Three variants: `logo.svg` (nav/horizontal), `logo-stacked.svg` (hero/footer), `logo-light.svg` (on dark backgrounds)
- The infinity mark uses `#b89a6a` gold — do not change this colour
- Minimum display size: 28px height (horizontal), 60px height (stacked)
- Do not distort, recolour, or add drop shadows

## Photography guidelines (for real images)
- Architectural: exteriors, atriums, façades — avoid interiors with visible people
- Colour grade: slightly cool, neutral — no Instagram warmth or heavy vignette
- Cropping: portrait `3:4` for project cards, `16:9` for hero/banner use
- File: `.jpg` optimised, max 300 KB per card image
